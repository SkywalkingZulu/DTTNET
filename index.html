Who we are.

DTT NETWORKS UNDER THE LEADERSHIP OF MR SANDILE DLAMINI IS A BBBEE COMPANY THAT DEALS WITH DIGITAL TELEVISION SOLUTIONS FROM TRAINING TO INSTALLATION AND SUPPORT. Sandile Dlamini graduated with Diploma in Journalism and for years worked for the then IBA now ICASA as a researcher and media monitor. Also worked SAPA and ABC News channel supplying news to Gabs fm and Kaya fm. While at ICASA he formed part of the team researching about Digital Migration and its success.

What we do

OPPORTUNITIES OFFERED THROUGH DTT DTT NETWORKS OFFERS South Africans the opportunity to be qualified artisans to service communities across the country in the migration to digital broadcast.

DTT history

Not only do our training cover DTT but covers all digital TV installation including XtraView-3RD viewing and DSTV. We also provide support for signal distribution as well as skills development
